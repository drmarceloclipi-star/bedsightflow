# Design System: Colors

The color palette is fundamental to the "Claude" aesthetic: muted, warm, and distinctly non-aggressive. We avoid harsh, highly saturated primary colors, favoring earthy tones, off-whites, and soft grays.

## Background Colors

- **App Background (Light)**: `#FDFBF7` or `#F9F8F6` — A warm, paper-like off-white. Avoids eye-strain associated with pure `#FFFFFF`.
- **Surface/Card (Light)**: `#FFFFFF` — True white is used to slightly elevate components off the warm background, creating subtle depth without shadows.
- **App Background (Dark)**: `#18181B` (Zinc-900) or `#1E1E1E` — Deep, muted slate rather than absolute black (`#000000`).
- **Surface/Card (Dark)**: `#27272A` (Zinc-800) — Slightly elevated dark gray.

## Text Colors

- **Primary Text**: `#27272A` (Light Mode) / `#E4E4E7` (Dark Mode) — High contrast but softer than pure black/white, leading to less visual fatigue.
- **Secondary Text**: `#71717A` (Light Mode) / `#A1A1AA` (Dark Mode) — Used for metadata, timestamps, and subtle hints where focus isn't strictly required.
- **Muted Text / Disabled**: `#A1A1AA` (Light Mode) / `#52525B` (Dark Mode).

## Accents & Brand Colors

Accent colors are used sparingly to guide the eye or indicate specific states (e.g., patient status, system alerts).

- **Primary Brand (Muted Terracotta/Orange)**: `#D97757` or `#C25B3D` (Provides warmth and acts as primary CTA).
- **Secondary Brand (Soft Sand)**: `#E6DFD3`
- **Success (Soft Muted Green)**: `#507A5E`
- **Warning (Soft Yellow/Amber)**: `#D9A05B`
- **Error (Soft Red/Rust)**: `#C25450`

## Borders & Dividers

- **Light Mode Borders**: `#E5E5E5` or `#E4E4E7` (Zinc-200 / Neutral-200) — Very subtle line work used to define boundaries only when negative space isn't enough.
- **Dark Mode Borders**: `#3F3F46` (Zinc-700) or `#27272A` (Zinc-800).
